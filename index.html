The purpose of MaaP is to provide a framework to create web interfaces for administration of business data stack-based on Node.js and MongoDB. The goal is to simplify the implementation process of such interfaces that the developer, leaning to the productivity of the framework MAAP, can generate in a simple and fast way, obtaining a considerable saving of time and effort.

The final user-generated pages will ultimately be the business expert who will be able to view, manage and change the various entities and data residing in MongoDB. MaaP is an acronym for MongoDB as an admin Platform.
